Who We Are

Established in 2001, we exist to increase and improve the opportunities and experiences of at-risk young people in Islington. Our work is both diverse and impactful. When we aren't running outreach sessions to engage with teenagers at-risk of gang violence, we're teaching 8 year olds how to make nutritious meals.
